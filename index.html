Our Mission

Raising awareness about domestic violence and educating students on ways to support survivors with empathy and advocacy.

Running donation drives to collect essential items like toiletries, clothes, and care packages for women in shelters.

Creating support programs that foster empowerment, healing, and long-term recovery for survivors of domestic violence.

Monthly meetings focused on education, awareness, and planning impactful initiatives.
Volunteer events and outreach opportunities to directly assist local shelters.
Student-led campaigns to amplify survivor voices and advocate for change.

About the Shelter

FemAid partners with local domestic violence shelters to provide resources, donations, and emotional support. Together, we work to help women and children find stability and healing in safe environments.

About 180 Turning Lives Around

180 Turning Lives Around is a nonprofit organization that helps survivors of domestic and sexual violence in Monmouth County, NJ.

They provide 24/7 hotlines, safe housing, crisis counseling, and advocacy.

180 also offers youth prevention programs and legal support.

FemAid partners with 180 to provide essential items, spread awareness, and support healing for women and children.
